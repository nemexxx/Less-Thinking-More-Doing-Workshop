Improvisation Workshops

Emely's improvisation workshops are hands-on and high-energy. People come to her workshops nervous and leave surprised by how much they laughed, how little they overthought, and how good it felt to just try.

This isn’t about being funny or perfect, but about:

  • Training Confidence
  • Speaking before Overthinking
  • Be Creative
  • Having Fun and Connecting with others

Emely designs every exercise to gently push past hesitation. The result? Participants feel lighter, louder, and a little more themselves.

Why choose Emely as your Host?

Emely knows how it feels to overthink and stay quiet.
She has felt it herself many times and seen it in over 150 participants. That’s why she creates an atmosphere where people feel safe to speak up and try something new. She encourages each participant to step outside their comfort zone.

Her workshops are shaped by her experience in theatre, business, and mindfulness. She’s spent 10 years on stage, studied at Germany’s leading business school WHU, helped build three startups, and nearly burned out trying to keep up. That’s when she found meditation and yoga to slow down and find clarity.

Today, she brings all of that into the room: energy, structure, presence, and a deep respect for people who dare to step outside their comfort zone.
